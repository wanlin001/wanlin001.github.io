Research

My research focuses on understanding tectonic processes through integrated approaches combining structural geology, seismology, and numerical modeling. I am particularly interested in the deformation mechanisms of fold-and-thrust belts and subduction zones.

Stress Heterogeneity in Subduction Zones

Northern Manila Subduction Zone

Investigation of stress heterogeneity within the subducting slab through analysis of earthquake doublets. This research reveals important implications for mantle rheology and subduction dynamics.

Southwestern Taiwan

Study of how lithological and structural factors influence crustal deformation in mudstone-rich fold-thrust belts, with implications for understanding tectonic evolution and seismic hazards.
